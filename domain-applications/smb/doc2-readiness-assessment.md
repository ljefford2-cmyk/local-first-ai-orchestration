*Know where you stand before you buy anything.*

| **What this is** This is a structured self-evaluation you complete before talking to any vendor, consultant, or IT provider. It takes 20–30 minutes. Check all boxes that apply. Write in answers where prompted. Tally your score on the Summary Scorecard (page 2) when you are done. |

| **☐** | Identify every AI tool in use across all staff — not just the ones you know about. Survey your team. |
